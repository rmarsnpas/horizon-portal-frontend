Privacy Statement

At Horizon House, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the information you share with us. This statement explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details (name, phone, email) submitted through inquiry forms.
  • Application details shared during admissions inquiries.
  • Basic website usage analytics (page visits, device type).

We do not collect sensitive health information through this website.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to admissions inquiries and schedule tours or calls.
  • To provide program information and updates.
  • To improve website functionality and user experience.

We do not sell or share your information with third parties.

Confidentiality

Member and staff identities are protected. Photos used on the site are selected to respect privacy. Any testimonials are published only with consent.

Data Security

Forms are transmitted securely. Access to inquiry data is limited to Horizon House staff. We take reasonable steps to protect against unauthorized access.

Your Rights

  • You may request that we delete your inquiry information.
  • You may opt out of email communications at any time.
  • You may ask what information we hold about you.
