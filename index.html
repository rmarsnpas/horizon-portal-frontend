Why Horizon House

We believe structure and compassion can coexist. Our team blends building safety expertise with recovery-minded care to create a place where people feel safe, respected, and supported.

Two Houses, One Mission: Horizon House operates two separate, gender-specific facilities—His House for men and Her House for women. This ensures a comfortable, focused environment where residents can concentrate on their recovery journey with peers who share similar experiences.
