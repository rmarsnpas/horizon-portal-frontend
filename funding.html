Funding & Insurance Options

We work with families, individuals, and referring professionals to find a funding path that works — because cost should never be the barrier to recovery.

Payment Options

We believe everyone deserves access to quality sober living. We offer several options to make placement possible.

Private Pay Self-pay accepted — contact us for current rates
Scholarships A limited number of scholarships become available to qualified individuals
Sliding Scale Considered case-by-case for those who qualify

For questions about scholarships, sliding scale availability, or current private pay rates, reach out directly — we'll work through every option with you.

Medi-Cal

Horizon House is an approved provider for Medi-Cal through the following managed care programs. If you or your client has active Medi-Cal coverage, we can work directly with your plan to verify eligibility and coordinate placement.

RUHS Riverside University Health System
IEHP Inland Empire Health Plan
HHope Housing HHope Supportive Housing Program

Not sure if your plan is covered? Contact us and we'll do a complimentary benefits verification for you.

Common Questions

How do I check if Medi-Cal will cover placement?
Check with your case worker first — they can confirm your active Medi-Cal coverage and which managed care plan you're enrolled in. You can also call us at (760) 766-0300 and we'll help verify eligibility on our end, usually within one business day.
Is prior authorization required for Medi-Cal?
Yes — a referral is required before prior authorization can be requested. Your case worker can provide this referral. Once we receive it, our team will handle the prior auth submission and follow up with your plan. If you're unsure how to get a referral, your case worker is the right person to contact first.
How do scholarships work?
A limited number of scholarship beds become available periodically. These are allocated based on financial need and program fit. Contact us to get on the list and we'll reach out when one opens up.
What does sliding scale mean?
Sliding scale means we adjust the monthly rate based on your documented income and ability to pay. It is considered on a case-by-case basis and is not guaranteed, but we try to work with people whenever we can.
I'm a referring professional. Who should I contact?
For male referrals, contact Marshall at (626) 616-7273 or mars@horizon-house.org. For female referrals, contact Liz at (818) 916-2214 or liz@horizon-house.org. You can also visit our For Professionals page for more info.
