Our Mission

We believe structure and compassion can coexist. Our team blends building safety expertise with recovery-minded care to create a place where people feel safe, respected, and supported.

Two Houses, One Mission: Horizon House operates two separate facilities — His House and Her House. This structure provides comfortable, focused environments where members can concentrate on their recovery journey with supportive peers.
