The Professionals Portal

A secure, dedicated workspace where referring professionals collaborate with Horizon House — track referrals, get real-time updates, and stay connected with your clients' recovery journey.

Planned Features

Priority 📋

Online Referral Submission

Submit a referral directly from the portal with client details, insurance info, and clinical notes — no phone tag required.

Priority 📡

Real-Time Referral Tracking

See where every referral stands — submitted, under review, admitted, or pending documentation — at a glance.

Priority 🏠

Bed Availability Dashboard

Check current openings at His House and Her House before you make a referral call — live availability, updated daily.

Planned 💬

Secure Messaging

Communicate directly with Marshall or Liz through an encrypted, HIPAA-conscious messaging thread tied to each client record.

Planned 📈

Client Progress Updates

Receive structured progress notes — engagement, attendance, behavioral flags, and milestone check-ins — delivered to your portal or inbox.

Planned 📁

Secure Document Sharing

Upload authorizations, clinical summaries, and discharge documents — and receive signed agreements and intake forms — all in one secure place.

Planned 🤝

Collaborative Care Planning

Co-author aftercare and step-down plans with the Horizon House team, so the transition from your care to ours is seamless and documented.

Planned 🔔

Custom Notification Preferences

Choose how and when you hear from us — instant alerts for critical events, daily digests, or weekly summaries. Your workflow, your way.

Planned 📊

Referral History & Outcomes

A full log of every client you've referred — length of stay, completion status, and follow-up notes — to support your own program evaluation.

Priority 🧪

Drug Screen Results

View results from your client's random drug screening tests — date, substances tested, and outcome — logged and accessible as they occur.

Priority 📅

Meeting Attendance Records

See submitted meeting attendance cards — which meetings were attended, how often, and any gaps — giving you a clear picture of your client's program engagement.

Planned ⚠️

Incident Reports

Receive structured reports for any incidents involving your client — behavioral concerns, rule violations, or safety events — documented and shared with you promptly.

How It Will Work

1

Request Access

Verified professionals apply for a portal account. We confirm your organization and set up your secure profile.

2

Submit a Referral

Fill out a structured referral form with client details, clinical background, and insurance information. Attach documents directly.

3

We Review & Respond

Horizon House reviews the referral and responds through the portal — admission approval, questions, or alternative suggestions — typically within 24 hours.

4

Track Progress in Real Time

Once your client is admitted, you receive structured updates through your portal dashboard and notification preferences.

5

Collaborate on the Care Plan

Use shared care planning tools to coordinate step-downs, aftercare, and long-term goals between your team and ours.
