About Horizon House

A structured, caring sober living community in the Coachella Valley — where recovery, community, and purpose come together.

Our Mission

We believe structure and compassion can coexist. Our team blends building safety expertise with recovery-minded care to create a place where people feel safe, respected, and supported.

Two Houses, One Mission: Horizon House operates two separate facilities — His House and Her House. This structure provides comfortable, focused environments where all members can concentrate on their recovery journey with supportive peers. We welcome all individuals in recovery and work with each person to find the best fit for their needs and comfort.

Our Values

Stability — A calm, orderly environment where recovery can take root

Accountability — Respectful, consistent expectations for every Member

Community — A supportive network that strengthens each individual

Growth — Daily habits that build long-term success

Integrity — Honest communication and responsible living

Hope — Because every day offers a new horizon

Our Commitment

Horizon House is committed to providing a safe, structured, and supportive sober living environment where Members can rebuild their lives with dignity and purpose. We believe in the power of community, the strength of routine, and the resilience of every person who walks through our doors.

We are a colony built for recovery — where there is hope on the horizon!
