Our Philosophy

At Horizon House, we believe that recovery is a journey, not a destination. Our structured sober living environment provides the foundation you need to build a new life free from addiction.

What We Offer

  • Safe, substance-free living environment
  • Peer support and accountability
  • Life skills development
  • Employment assistance
  • Weekly house meetings
  • Drug screening program
  • Connection to recovery resources
  • 24/7 support and guidance

Our Commitment

We provide a structured, supportive environment where members can focus on their recovery while rebuilding their lives. Our house managers are dedicated to helping each member succeed.

House Standards

  • Zero tolerance for substance use
  • Mandatory attendance at recovery meetings
  • Regular drug testing
  • Respect for all members and property
  • Active participation in house activities
  • Employment or active job search required
