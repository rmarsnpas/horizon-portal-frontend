Our commitment to making this site usable by everyone.

Accessibility Statement

Horizon House is committed to ensuring our website is accessible to all visitors, including people with disabilities. We strive to meet or exceed the requirements of the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1, Level AA).

Our Accessibility Efforts

  • Using clear headings and semantic HTML for screen readers.
  • Providing descriptive alt text for all meaningful images.
  • Ensuring sufficient color contrast between text and backgrounds.
  • Making all forms and navigation usable by keyboard.
  • Designing responsive layouts for mobile and desktop devices.

Ongoing Improvements

We regularly review our site with automated tools and manual checks to identify and fix accessibility issues. As our program grows, we will continue to improve accessibility features.

Feedback

If you encounter any accessibility barriers on our site, please let us know so we can address them promptly.
